What is AI Design?

AI Design is the use of artificial intelligence to enhance and automate design processes across UI/UX, product design, branding, and content creation. AI can:

  • Predict what users want before they ask
  • Optimize designs based on A/B testing data
  • Auto-generate layouts, visuals, and themes
  • Personalize interfaces in real-time
  • Analyze design effectiveness with heatmaps and behavior tracking

At Prabony AI, we merge human creativity with machine intelligence to create stunning, functional, and adaptable designs.
