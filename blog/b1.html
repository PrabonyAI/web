What is AI Automation?

AI Automation combines artificial intelligence with software automation to perform tasks that would otherwise require human involvement. At Prabony AI, we leverage:

  • Machine Learning to enable systems to learn and improve
  • Natural Language Processing (NLP) for smart text/email/chat handling
  • Computer Vision to interpret images and visuals
  • Robotic Process Automation (RPA) for workflow efficiency

Together, these technologies power intelligent bots, agents, and systems that boost performance across departments.
