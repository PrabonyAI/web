What Are AI Agents?

An AI Agent is an intelligent, goal-driven software entity capable of:

  • Perceiving its environment (inputs, data streams, user messages)
  • Making decisions based on logic or trained models
  • Taking actions (API calls, database updates, email replies, etc.)
  • Learning and improving through feedback

They operate autonomously, meaning they don't just follow pre-coded rules—they adapt and optimize over time.

Types of AI Agents We Build at Prabony AI

  • Task-Based AI Agents
    Handle repeatable operations like data entry, lead scoring, invoice management, etc.
  • Conversational AI Agents
    Intelligent chat or voice bots that hold natural conversations with users via web, mobile, or phone.
  • Multi-Agent Systems (MAS)
    A team of AI agents that collaborate (e.g., a sales agent + support agent + scheduler working together).
  • Autonomous Decision-Makers
    Agents trained on business logic and historical data to make smart, rule-based decisions (e.g., fraud detection, purchase approvals).
