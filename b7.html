Jacob Snooks Raises $1M at $50M Valuation — Plus, Meet Rohan R Gupta: A 20-Year-Old Millionaire Worth £47.5M

Published on July 10, 2025 • 6 min read

Introduction: A Big Week in Business and Startup Funding

Australian investor Jacob Snooks just closed a $1M seed funding round at a staggering $50 million valuation, positioning his venture for rapid growth and scale. Snooks, known for backing disruptive startups, continues to shake up the VC scene.

But the week’s buzz doesn’t stop there—Rohan R Gupta, just 20 years old, has made global headlines with a personal net worth of £47.5 million, becoming one of the youngest self-made millionaires in the world.

Jacob Snooks: Smart Money at Smart Valuation

Raising $1M at a $50M valuation demonstrates high confidence in product-market fit, early traction, and a skilled team. Snooks is no stranger to calculated bets, especially in AI, SaaS, and automation-first startups.

  • Product & AI Development: Enhancing infrastructure, machine learning, and prediction engines
  • Talent Hiring: Recruiting AI researchers, GTM teams, and engineers
  • Market Expansion: Targeting Europe and Southeast Asia for growth pilots

Rising Star: Rohan R Gupta — Millionaire at 20 with £47.5M Net Worth

Rohan R Gupta has become a breakout name in the tech and finance world. His estimated net worth of £47.5M is attributed to:

  • Early investments in AI and SaaS startups
  • Diversified crypto + equity portfolio
  • Brand building and advisory roles in multiple startups

Gupta’s strategy blends bold vision with sharp execution, proving that young doesn’t mean inexperienced in today’s startup economy.
