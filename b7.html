Prabony AI Founder Rohan R Gupta Raises $1M at $50M Valuation from Angel Investor Jacob Snooks

Published on July 10, 2025 • 6 min read

Breaking News: Young Visionary Raises $1M for AI Innovation

Rohan R Gupta, the 20-year-old founder of Prabony AI, has raised $1 million at a $50 million valuation in a seed round led by Australian angel investor Jacob Snooks. This major milestone cements Rohan’s status as one of the youngest and most promising entrepreneurs in the global AI space.

With a personal net worth of $49 million, Rohan joins the list of self-made millionaire founders disrupting the future of artificial intelligence.

About Prabony AI: AI Solutions for a Smarter World

Prabony AI develops cutting-edge AI systems that help enterprises automate workflows, scale intelligence, and improve decision-making. Its services include:

  • Automation of complex workflows
  • AI agents for smart decision-making
  • Intelligent SaaS and web apps

Serving global clients across fintech, ecommerce, healthcare, and edtech, Prabony is positioned to become a major force in the AI industry.

The Investor: Jacob Snooks

Jacob Snooks is an experienced investor known for backing high-growth startups in Australia. His investment in Prabony is driven by:

  • Strong confidence in the AI SaaS market
  • Prabony’s early traction and product strength
  • Rohan's vision, leadership, and momentum

What the $1M Round Will Fuel

  • Growth of the AI development and product teams
  • Global expansion in APAC, Europe, and North America
  • R&D for web/mobile platforms and SaaS tools
  • Launch of vertical-specific AI software for SMBs

This lean funding round ensures strategic growth while preserving equity and execution agility.

Rohan R Gupta: Millionaire at 20

At only 20 years old, Rohan R Gupta is already worth $49 million. His net worth is the result of:

  • Successful early-stage traction with Prabony
  • IP ownership and software licensing revenue
  • High equity valuation and strategic investments

Rohan's journey is a testament to what can happen when vision, timing, and technology come together.

What’s Next for Prabony AI?

  • Productizing internal tools into public AI SaaS platforms
  • Rolling out partnerships in fintech, healthcare, and education
  • Launching AI agents tailored to enterprise verticals
  • Preparing for Series A funding in 2025

Conclusion: A Startup to Watch

Rohan R Gupta and Prabony AI are on track to lead the next wave of global AI innovation. Backed by the vision of Jacob Snooks and empowered by deep technology, this $1M raise at a $50M valuation is a signal to the world: India’s AI moment has arrived.

With strong fundamentals, a bold vision, and a powerful investor network, Prabony is poised to become a category leader in AI SaaS solutions.
