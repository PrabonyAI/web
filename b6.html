What is AI SaaS?

AI SaaS (Artificial Intelligence as a Service) combines the scalability of cloud-based platforms with the intelligence of AI technologies like:

  • Machine Learning for real-time predictions and automations
  • Natural Language Processing for chatbots, summarization, and smart input
  • Computer Vision for recognition, detection, and automation
  • Generative AI for content creation, personalization, and conversation

AI SaaS solutions are delivered through the web and accessible to users without requiring installations—just smarter experiences on demand.
